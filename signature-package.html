Common Questions

Answers to the obvious questions.

Is there a minimum contract length?

The retainer is month-to-month after the initial strategy month. We don't lock you into long-term contracts. That said, social media is a compounding investment — the results get significantly stronger at the 3–6 month mark. We recommend committing to at least 3 months to see meaningful results.

What does the onboarding process look like?

After signing, you complete a detailed onboarding form and schedule your 60-minute strategy call. From there, we handle everything. Your first content calendar is delivered for approval before anything goes live.

Do I have to approve content before it goes live?

Yes. Every content calendar is submitted for your review and approval before publishing. You'll have a clear window to request edits or provide feedback. We aim to make this process as frictionless as possible.

What if I want to start with the Blueprint before committing to the retainer?

That's exactly what the Instagram Growth Blueprint is designed for. You get the full strategy for $997, and if you decide to join the retainer within 30 days, the full $997 is credited to your first month. It's a zero-risk way to start.

What industries do you specialize in?

We work exclusively with service-based businesses where trust and expertise drive buying decisions. This includes med spas, dermatologists, surgeons, therapists, coaches, fitness studios, home builders, real estate professionals, and interior designers.
