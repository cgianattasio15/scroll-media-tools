FAQ

Common questions.

What does a Content Shoot actually look like? +
We come in with a shot list already locked and capture everything for all three pieces, plus your raw library, in one focused two-hour session. We handle creative direction and framing. You just show up and be present.
What exactly do I get? +
Two fully edited Reels, one designed carousel, your full raw photo and b-roll library, suggested captions for each piece, and a simple posting guide. One round of revisions is included on the three edited pieces.
How long does it take? +
About two to three weeks from kickoff to delivery, starting the moment the contract is signed. Four phases: plan, shoot, edit and deliver, then you post on your own schedule.
Do I need to provide anything for the shoot? +
Just your space, your team, and any products or props relevant to your brand. We send a short prep note before the day so you know what to have ready. We handle equipment and creative direction.
What about props, models, or paid talent? +
The Content Shoot is a one-time production package. Ongoing posting, scheduling, and day-to-day management live in the Signature Package. Any props, models, or paid talent get scoped together on the call.
Is Scroll Media based in Cincinnati? +
Yes. We're Cincinnati-based and shoots happen on-site at your location, primarily in the Greater Cincinnati area. Outside the area? Reach out and we'll talk options.
