## SECTION 1: The Updated Campaign Plan

This document supersedes `CAMPAIGN-LAUNCH-STRATEGY.md`. It reflects all strategic decisions and asset updates as of today.

### Campaign Goal

- **Primary:** 3–5 new Signature Package clients in 30–60 days.
- **Secondary:** Drive Blueprint and Playbook sales as a byproduct of top-of-funnel activity.

### The Funnel

**Audience-to-Action Playbook ($87) → Instagram Growth Blueprint ($997) → Signature Package ($2,750+/mo)**

- The **Playbook** is the lead qualifier. It filters for prospects willing to invest in strategy.
- The **Blueprint** is the trust-builder. It delivers immense value and proves our expertise, making the Signature Package the next logical step.
- The **Signature Package** is the destination. It’s the full-service solution for clients who are serious about growth.

### Campaign Flow

1.  **Pre-Launch (7 days):** Execute the 7-day content plan (`PRE-LAUNCH-CONTENT-PLAN.md`) to warm up the audience and build anticipation.
2.  **Launch Day:** Post the official launch announcement on all channels. Update Instagram bio link to point to the Signature Package page (`tools.scrollmedia.co/signature-package`).
3.  **Direct Outreach (Days 1–14):** Begin personalized outreach to the scored prospect list. Use the DM scripts provided below.
4.  **Nurture (Ongoing):** All Playbook buyers are automatically added to the 5-email Kit sequence. All page visitors who don’t convert are added to the retargeting audience (pending setup).
5.  **Conversion (Ongoing):** Book discovery calls for the Blueprint and Signature Package via the dedicated Calendly links.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Weekly:** Outreach messages sent, response rate, discovery calls booked.
- **Monthly:** Playbook sales, Blueprint sales, Signature Package closes.
- **Overall:** Cost per acquisition (CPA), conversion rate from discovery call to close.
