## Notes

- All three versions intentionally avoid any mention of pricing, services, or a sales ask.
- The "personality quiz" reference is the disarming hook — it lowers the perceived barrier to clicking.
- "No pitch on the other side" (Draft C) is the most trust-building line — consider adding it to whichever version is chosen.
- Subject lines are intentionally lowercase and conversational — they read like a message from a real person, not a marketing email.
