## The Strategy

This isn’t a typical “something is coming” launch. It’s a strategic pre-launch designed to solve problems for your audience *before* you ask them to buy anything. The content is the value. The campaign pages are the solution when they want more.

**The narrative arc for the week:**

1.  **Days 1–3: The Problem.** Agitate the core pain points your ICP faces (inconsistency, no ROI, content that doesn’t convert).
2.  **Days 4–5: The Framework.** Introduce the core Scroll Media philosophies (strategy first, funnels, authority building) as the solution.
3.  **Days 6–7: The System.** Reveal the Blueprint and Signature Package as the tangible, investable systems that implement the frameworks.

### Day 4: The Funnel-Aware Framework (LinkedIn Post)

**Post:**
> Your Instagram content is failing for one simple reason: you’re asking for the sale on the first date.
> 
> Not every follower is ready to buy. In fact, most aren’t.
> 
> The fix is to think like a funnel, not a megaphone.
> 
> **Top of Funnel (TOFU):** Attract your ICP. Speak to their problem. Don’t mention your offer.
> **Middle of Funnel (MOFU):** Educate and build trust. Show them *how* you solve the problem. Frame your process as the solution.
> **Bottom of Funnel (BOFU):** Convert high-intent prospects. This is where you make the ask — book a call, get a quote, buy the thing.
> 
> Most businesses spend 90% of their time at the bottom of the funnel, talking to 1% of their audience.
> 
> Flip the ratio. Spend 90% of your time at the top and middle. The bottom of the funnel will take care of itself.
> 
> This is the Funnel-Aware Framework we build for every client. It’s not complicated. But it works.
> 
> We’ve packaged the entire system into our Signature Package for service businesses ready to get serious about growth. Link in comments.
