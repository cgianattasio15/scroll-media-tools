Strategic Direction
Account narrative and Instagram's role for this client

Skin by Brownlee is a perimenopause-focused skincare brand founded by Sylvia Brownlee, a licensed esthetician with 17+ years of experience. The brand's core thesis: perimenopause skin is fundamentally different from general aging skin, and most skincare brands completely ignore it. Sylvia's clinical expertise and personal experience with perimenopause make her the most credible voice in this underserved niche.

The business is a national DTC e-commerce brand, with a strategic 2026 goal of fully transitioning away from in-person services to focus entirely on product sales and digital growth. The Skin Quiz is the primary lead capture mechanism — it personalizes the product recommendation, reduces purchase friction, and feeds the email list that drives repeat purchases.

Instagram's three-part job: (1) establish Sylvia as the go-to perimenopause skin authority and drive new audience discovery among women 40–58 experiencing skin changes they don't understand; (2) build trust through education — what's happening to perimenopause skin, why standard products fail, how to build the right routine — and drive Skin Quiz completions as the primary MOFU conversion; (3) convert quiz-takers and warm followers into e-commerce product purchases through specific product CTAs and social proof.

At Rise stage with ~33K followers, the account has strong reach and CTR. The strategic priority is deepening the educational content engine (Sylvia's expertise is the moat) while sharpening the Skin Quiz CTA to convert the warm audience into email subscribers and first-time buyers.

IVP Profile
Ideal Viewer / Prospect — who we're talking to and what they need
Demographics
  • Women, ages 40–58, national (US)
  • In perimenopause or early menopause transition
  • Household income $60K–$150K+
  • Educated, health-conscious, values-driven consumers
  • Active on Instagram; follows wellness, skincare, women's health content
Psychographics
  • Frustrated that her current skincare routine stopped working
  • Feels underserved by mainstream beauty brands that ignore her age group
  • Wants clinical expertise, not generic "anti-aging" marketing
  • Values transparency — wants to understand ingredients and why they work
  • Trusts personal stories and clinical credentials over celebrity endorsements
Core Pain Points
  • Unexplained skin changes — dryness, sensitivity, breakouts she didn't have before
  • Wasted money — products that worked before no longer work
  • Ignored by the industry — no brands specifically address her skin phase
  • Information overload — conflicting advice, no trusted expert for her specific situation
Dream Outcome

Tangible: A personalized skincare routine that actually works for her perimenopause skin — products she understands, from a brand built specifically for her. Skin that feels healthy, balanced, and like hers again.

Emotional: Finally feels seen and understood by a brand. Trusts Sylvia's expertise the way she trusts a doctor. Confident in her routine and proud to recommend it to every woman in her circle going through the same thing.

Counter-Positioning
vs. Mass Skincare (CeraVe, Neutrogena)

Perimenopause-specific formulation vs. generic "sensitive skin" positioning. Clinical esthetician expertise vs. dermatologist-tested marketing. Built for this specific hormonal phase — not just moisturizing.

vs. Luxury Anti-Aging (La Mer, Estée Lauder)

Accessible price point with clinical efficacy. Founder-led transparency vs. corporate brand mystique. Perimenopause-specific education that luxury brands don't provide.

Skin by Brownlee Advantage

17+ years esthetician expertise + personal perimenopause experience + Skin Quiz personalization + founder vulnerability and authentic storytelling no mass brand can replicate.
