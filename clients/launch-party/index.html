Strategic Direction
Account narrative and Instagram's role for this client

Launch Party is Cincinnati's premier indie and clean beauty boutique, located in Over-the-Rhine. They curate an intentional selection of independent, sustainable, and founder-led beauty brands that can't be found at Sephora, Ulta, or Target — making discovery and curation their core value proposition.

The business operates both in-store (OTR) and via e-commerce at shoplaunchparty.com, with a growing national online customer base. The founder's deep knowledge of ingredients, brand stories, and clean beauty philosophy is the single biggest differentiator — this is not a shelf-stocking retailer, it's an expert-led curation service.

Instagram's role for Launch Party is threefold: (1) drive brand discovery and grow the digital community among women who value indie beauty and don't know Launch Party exists yet; (2) build trust through education — ingredients, brand stories, how-to content — that makes the IVP feel informed and confident before buying; (3) convert that trust into e-commerce sales and in-store visits through specific product CTAs and local community moments.

At Rise stage with 32,866 followers, Launch Party has strong reach and conversion signals. The strategic priority is maintaining the TOFU engine while sharpening BOFU content to drive more direct e-comm revenue from the existing warm audience.

IVP Profile
Ideal Viewer / Prospect — who we're talking to and what they need
Demographics
  • Women, ages 22–40
  • Urban/suburban Cincinnati and national e-comm buyers
  • Household income $50K–$120K+
  • Values-driven consumers — clean, indie, sustainable
  • Active on Instagram; beauty content is a core interest
Psychographics
  • Rejects mass-market beauty — wants to discover, not just buy
  • Ingredient-conscious — reads labels, researches brands
  • Community-driven — supports local and founder-led businesses
  • Aesthetics-aware — packaging, brand story, and values matter
  • Skeptical of big-box beauty — wants curation, not overwhelm
Core Pain Points
  • Overwhelm — too many products, no trusted guide
  • Greenwashing fatigue — can't tell what's actually clean
  • Discovery gap — doesn't know indie brands exist
  • Price justification — indie beauty costs more, needs proof of value
Dream Outcome

Tangible: A curated beauty routine she trusts completely — products that work, ingredients she understands, brands she's proud to support. A go-to store (online and in-person) she recommends to every friend.

Emotional: Feels like she's finally found her beauty tribe. Buying from Launch Party feels intentional and aligned with her values — not just a transaction. Proud to support indie founders and local business.

Counter-Positioning
vs. Sephora / Ulta

Curated indie selection vs. overwhelming inventory. Expert-led guidance vs. commission-driven floor staff. Every brand has a story and a reason for being on the shelf.

vs. Target / Mass Market

Ingredient transparency and clean standards vs. greenwashing. Founder-led brands vs. corporate beauty. Discovery-first shopping experience vs. algorithm-driven shelves.

vs. Online DTC Brands

Multi-brand curation means you find your whole routine in one place. Expert guidance removes the trial-and-error cost. Local OTR roots create community that DTC brands can't replicate.
