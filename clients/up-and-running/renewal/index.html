The ROI Case

How We Think About Return

Social media ROI for a trust-based, community-driven business like Up and Running isn't measured in clicks alone. Here's how we track and project the real financial impact.

The Trust Infrastructure Model

For a business like Up and Running, sales are built on trust, not clicks. The path from someone seeing a reel to walking into your store or buying online is non-linear — but it's measurable.

We track High-Intent Actions (HIAs) — specifically link taps — as the primary signal that someone has moved from passive viewer to active consideration. We then project the financial value of those actions based on your average customer value.
