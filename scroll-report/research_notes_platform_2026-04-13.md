# Platform research notes for Scroll Report — 2026-04-13

## Source 1: Meta Newsroom — Instagram Expands Teen Accounts Inspired by 13+ Content Ratings
- Date: 2026-04-09
- URL: https://about.fb.com/news/2026/04/instagram-expands-teen-accounts-inspired-by-13-content-ratings/
- Core update: Instagram is internationally expanding the updated Teen Accounts default 13+ content setting and adding a stricter parent-controlled Limited Content mode.
- Specific product changes:
  - Teens under 18 are automatically put into the updated 13+ setting and cannot opt out without parent permission.
  - Search blocks expand to more mature terms including alcohol and gore, including misspellings.
  - Teens cannot follow or interact with accounts repeatedly sharing age-inappropriate content; those accounts also cannot DM or comment on teen posts.
  - Potentially age-inappropriate content is more aggressively filtered across recommendations, feed, stories, comments, and DM links.
  - Meta says AI experiences for teens are being constrained to avoid age-inappropriate responses.
- Strategic angle for Scroll Report:
  - Discovery and visibility rules are becoming more segmented by audience age and account safety profile.
  - Brands in youth-adjacent categories need cleaner metadata, safer creative framing, and fewer ambiguous references that could trigger reduced discoverability.
  - Search visibility is no longer universal; what is searchable for adults may be suppressed for younger audiences.

## Source 2: Variety — Instagram Reels Trending Ads Adds TV & Movies, Cultural Moments Like NFL Games
- Date: 2026-03-26
- URL: https://variety.com/2026/digital/news/instagram-reels-trending-ads-tv-movies-cultural-moments-nfl-1236698010/
- Core update: Instagram is expanding Reels Trending Ads inventory and Meta is extending Advantage+ creative video tools.
- Specific product changes:
  - Reels Trending Ads now include new categories such as TV & movies, travel, business, and finance and investments.
  - Meta is launching limited-content lineups for cultural moments like Fashion Week, Formula 1, Black Friday, and NFL games.
  - Reserve-buying test lets advertisers capture high-attention windows of up to 24 hours.
  - Advantage+ creative can generate UGC-style videos from image assets using avatars and voiceovers.
  - Meta is expanding AI voiceover translation and text-overlay translation for advertisers.
  - Catalog advertisers can auto-transform product catalogs into immersive Reels video ads.
  - Creator Marketplace gets stronger audience filtering for better creator-brand matching.
- Quantitative claim reported by Variety:
  - Meta says Reels Trending Ads drove an incremental 6.6 percentage point ad-recall lift across 59 internal studies.
  - Meta says Reels catalog product video campaigns had 33% higher incremental conversions than campaigns without those placements.
- Strategic angle for Scroll Report:
  - Reels is becoming a more intentional bridge between cultural relevance and conversion, not just organic reach.
  - Faster localization and UGC-style generation lowers production friction for paid creative.
  - For organic teams, the signal is that culture-timed content and creator-style formats are increasingly shaping both paid and organic performance expectations.

- Date: 2026-03-18.
- URL: https://almcorp.com/blog/instagram-clickable-links-post-captions-meta-verified/

- Specific product details:

  - Users can publish up to 10 caption-linked posts per month.

- Strategic angle for Scroll Report:
  - Instagram is selectively opening more direct-response behavior when it deepens creator monetization or subscription value.
  - Creator-led distribution may become more commercially measurable, especially for affiliate, newsletter, and offer-led campaigns.
  - Brands should treat creator partnerships as an increasingly important commerce layer even if the feature is not yet brand-native.
