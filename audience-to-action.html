The Problem

You're posting. You're not converting.

You're creating content. Your audience is watching. But they're not doing anything. That's not a content problem — it's a system problem. Here's what's breaking down.

📉

Weak CTAs That Don't Convert

Generic "link in bio" and "DM me" CTAs are invisible to your audience. They don't create urgency, don't signal value, and don't tell your viewer what to do next.

🎯

Wrong CTA for the Wrong Stage

Asking cold followers to book a call is like proposing on a first date. Without funnel-stage alignment, your CTAs push people away instead of pulling them in.

💬

DMs That Go Nowhere

Someone DMs you. You respond. The conversation dies. Without a structured DM system, warm leads slip through the cracks every single day.

The Framework

Every post has a job. Most don't know what it is.

The Funnel-Aware CTA Framework maps every content format to the right funnel stage — so your CTAs match where your viewer actually is in their decision journey.

TOFU · Top of Funnel

Attract & Educate

Cold audience. They don't know you yet. Your job is to earn their attention and give them a reason to follow.

  • Reels with broad reach
  • Educational carousels
  • Trend-adjacent content
Goal: Save, Follow, Share
MOFU · Middle of Funnel

Build Trust & Nurture

Warm audience. They follow you. Your job is to deepen the relationship and move them toward a decision.

  • Behind-the-scenes content
  • Case studies & results
  • FAQ and objection content
Goal: DM, Comment, Click
BOFU · Bottom of Funnel

Convert & Close

Hot audience. They're ready. Your job is to make the next step obvious, frictionless, and urgent.

  • Offer-specific content
  • Social proof & testimonials
  • Limited availability posts
Goal: Book, Buy, Apply
