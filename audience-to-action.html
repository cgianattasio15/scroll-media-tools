What's Inside

4 frameworks that build a complete conversion system

Each framework is a standalone tool. Together, they form a complete Instagram strategy you can implement this week.

Framework 01

The Content Funnel Map

Most businesses post the same type of content over and over — usually educational or entertaining — but never move anyone toward a purchase. This framework maps your content to three funnel stages (Awareness, Consideration, Decision) so every post has a purpose.

Example: A fitness studio uses Awareness posts to show transformation results, Consideration posts to explain their methodology, and Decision posts with a direct "Book a free class" CTA. Each stage does a different job.
Framework 02

The Hook Formula

You have 1.7 seconds to stop the scroll. Most hooks are either too generic ("5 tips for...") or too clever (no one knows what you're talking about). This formula gives you a repeatable structure for writing hooks that stop the right person and filter out everyone else.

Example: Instead of "How to grow your Instagram," a med spa writes: "Why your Instagram isn't booking consultations (and it's not your content)." Specific, targeted, and speaks directly to the pain point.
Framework 03

The CTA System

A call-to-action isn't just "link in bio." This system shows you how to match your CTA to the funnel stage of the post — so you're not asking cold followers to book a call before they trust you, and you're not leaving warm leads without a clear next step.

Example: A business coach uses "Save this for later" on Awareness posts, "Comment GUIDE and I'll send it to you" on Consideration posts, and "DM me READY to get started" on Decision posts. Each CTA is calibrated to where the viewer is in their journey.
Framework 04

The Conversion Content Calendar

A 4-week repeatable content structure that balances all three funnel stages, prevents content fatigue, and ensures you're always moving followers closer to a buying decision — without burning out or running out of ideas.

Example: Week 1: 2 Awareness + 1 Decision. Week 2: 1 Awareness + 1 Consideration + 1 Decision. The ratio shifts based on where your audience is in the buying cycle — not just what's trending.
