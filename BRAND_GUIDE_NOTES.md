# Scroll Media Brand Guide — Key Findings

## Priority KPIs (from Brand Guide p.7-8)
Scroll Media evaluates performance through a strategic lens, not vanity metrics.

**Priority metrics include:**
1. Watch time & average view duration — Retention signals quality and relevance
2. Saves & shares — Indicators of value and shareability
3. Profile visits — Interest and consideration signals
4. Link taps — Intent and action signals
5. Retention patterns — Where audiences drop off reveals what works and what doesn't

**NOT priority:** Likes, follower count (vanity metrics)

## Strategic Framework
- Attention is the entry point (first 1-3 seconds)
- Retention is the real signal (watch time, replays, saves, shares)
- Consistency beats virality
- Audience psychology over platform hacks
- Execution quality compounds

## Core Focus Niches
- Med Spas — Building trust and showcasing transformations
- Dermatologists, Doctors, & Surgeons — Positioning expertise and educating audiences
- Therapists & Physical Therapists — Demystifying services and building credibility
- Coaching Services — Establishing authority and attracting ideal clients
- Fitness Studios — Driving membership growth through community and differentiation
- Home Builders — Showcasing craftsmanship and building brand equity
- Real Estate Professionals — Creating personal brand and generating leads
- Interior Designers — Showcasing portfolio and attracting premium clients

## Signature Package (from Brand Guide p.9-10)
- Structure: 3-month initial term (Month 1 strategy development, Months 2-3 execution), followed by 6-month extensions
- Includes: 3 permanent feed posts/week, 2 story sequences/week, monthly content shoots, biweekly strategic calls, monthly performance reporting
- Posting: Mon/Wed/Fri recommended
- Stories: Tue/Thu recommended

## Brand Voice
- Direct, not academic
- Strategic, not fluffy
- Confident, not hype-driven
- Human, not corporate
- Tagline: "Media is the new word of mouth"
