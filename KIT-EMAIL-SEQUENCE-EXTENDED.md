## Email 5 — Day 35 After Purchase

**Subject:** A question for you

**Body:**
Hey {{ subscriber.first_name | default: "there" }},

I have a question for you, and I’d appreciate an honest answer.

What’s the one thing stopping you from having the Instagram presence you want for your business?

Is it…

1.  **Time?** (You know what to do, you just don’t have time to do it.)
2.  **Clarity?** (You’re not sure what to post or what will actually work.)
3.  **Consistency?** (You do it for a while, then fall off when things get busy.)
4.  **Confidence?** (You’re worried your content doesn’t look professional enough.)
5.  **ROI?** (You’re not convinced it will actually lead to new business.)

If you feel comfortable sharing, just reply with the number.

Your answer helps me understand where people are really getting stuck.

And if your answer is #1, #3, or #4 — that’s what the Signature Package solves. It’s a system for consistency, executed by a team that makes you look as professional as you are.

If it’s #2 or #5, that’s what the Blueprint is for. It gives you the clarity and the ROI case before you invest in execution.

Either way, there’s a path forward.

— Chase
