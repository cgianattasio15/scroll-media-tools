- **Top of Funnel (TOFU):** Attract new people. Job is to get reach and shares.

- **Bottom of Funnel (BOFU):** Convert followers into leads. Job is to get clicks and bookings.

You post a powerful client testimonial (a BOFU message) as a trending audio Reel (a TOFU format). The Reel gets tons of views from people who have no idea who you are. They aren’t ready for a testimonial, so they scroll by. The algorithm sees low engagement and stops showing your post.

You wasted your best message on an audience that wasn’t ready to hear it.

**Your action item:**

Look at the last piece of content you posted. Ask yourself: was the primary goal to attract, nurture, or convert? Did the format you chose align with that goal?

Just understanding this distinction puts you ahead of 90% of your competitors.

We’ve covered your bio and the Format-Funnel Mismatch.

Now let’s talk about the single most important line in every post: the Call to Action (CTA).

Most businesses use vague, weak CTAs like “Learn more” or “Link in bio.” This is like a salesperson ending a pitch with “So... what do you think?”

It’s a hope, not a strategy. A weak CTA doesn’t just fail to convert; it signals a lack of confidence in your own offer.

We use a **CTA Library** that gives every post one of two jobs:

**1. Start a Conversation (Engagement CTAs):** These are for your TOFU and MOFU content. The goal is to get comments, saves, and shares. Examples:
- “Save this for your next [relevant event].”
- “What’s the one thing you’re struggling with when it comes to [topic]?”

**2. Start a Conversion (Conversion CTAs):** These are for your BOFU content. The goal is to get a click that leads to a business outcome. Examples:
- “Tap the link in my bio to book your free consultation.”
- “Download my free [Lead Magnet] to solve [problem].”

As the book *Cashvertising* teaches, people are more motivated to **move away from a problem** than toward a benefit. Your CTA is the moment you offer them the escape route.

**Your action item:**

For this week, pick ONE primary CTA for your entire Instagram profile. If your goal is to book calls, every BOFU post should drive to your booking link. If your goal is to grow your email list, every MOFU post should drive to your lead magnet. Stop confusing your audience. Give them one clear path.
