🎯
Instagram Prioritizes Reels Engagement
Adam Mosseri emphasized that Instagram’s algorithm now heavily favors Reels with high engagement, including shares and saves, over traditional feed posts.
Why It Matters
Boutique service brands must pivot quickly to create engaging, short-form video content that encourages interaction to maintain and grow organic reach.
In Action — 2 Examples
Example 1
For a med spa: Produce a 30-second Reel demonstrating a popular treatment with before-and-after visuals and a call-to-action asking viewers to save it for their next appointment inspiration.
Example 2
For a fitness studio: Share a quick, high-energy workout tip Reel encouraging followers to tag a friend who needs motivation, boosting shares and engagement.
Coaching Question
"Which upcoming client campaigns can we repurpose or create as Reels to maximize shares and saves this week?"
⚡
Lucas O’Keefe Highlights Story Polls For Feedback
Lucas O’Keefe reported a significant lift in story engagement when using interactive polls and quizzes, recommending these as tools to source direct audience feedback.
Why It Matters
Boutique service brands can leverage story polls to better understand client preferences and tailor offerings, creating a more personalized brand experience that drives loyalty.
In Action — 2 Examples
Example 1
For a dental clinic: Use Instagram Story polls asking followers to vote on preferred appointment times or favorite whitening treatments, then share results with personalized tips.
Example 2
For a wellness coach: Run a quiz Story on common stress triggers followed by a poll on preferred relaxation techniques, then create content based on top answers.
Coaching Question
"What poll or quiz concepts can we test this week to gather actionable audience insights for our top clients?"
🔑
Brock Johnson Urges Focus on Authentic Creator Collabs
Brock Johnson stressed the effectiveness of partnering with micro-influencers who have authentic connections with niche audiences, rather than chasing follower counts.
Why It Matters
Boutique brands benefit from authentic, niche-specific influencer collaborations that drive higher conversion rates and build trust faster than broad influencer campaigns.
In Action — 2 Examples
Example 1
For a jewelry brand: Partner with a local fashion micro-influencer to create Reels showcasing how to style signature pieces for everyday wear.
Example 2
For a retail boutique: Collaborate with a neighborhood lifestyle creator to share behind-the-scenes shopping experiences, tagged with exclusive promo codes.
Coaching Question
"Which micro-influencers align with our clients’ niches and values for potential authentic collaborations this quarter?"
