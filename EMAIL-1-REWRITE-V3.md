Hey {{ subscriber.first_name | default: "there" }},

Let me guess. You’re posting on Instagram consistently. You’re trying to keep up with Reels, Stories, and carousels. You’re doing everything you’re “supposed to do.”

And you’re getting nothing from it.

No inquiries. No discovery calls. No clients.

This is the #1 frustration I hear from the 200+ service business owners I’ve audited. And 9 times out of 10, the problem isn’t your content. It’s your profile.

When a high-intent prospect lands on your profile, they make a 3-second decision: is this for me, or not? Your profile isn’t a social media page — it’s your new homepage. It’s a silent sales page that’s either converting visitors or turning them away.

Here’s a 5-point audit you can do in the next 10 minutes to fix it.

**1. Your Bio**
Does it say who you help and what outcome you deliver? Not just what you do.
- Bad: “Interior designer | Cincinnati, OH”
- Good: “Helping Cincinnati homeowners design spaces they actually want to come home to”

**2. Your Link in Bio**
Is it a single, clear call to action? Or is it a Linktree with 15 different options? Give them one door to walk through — the one that leads to a business outcome (booking a call, downloading a lead magnet, etc.).

**3. Your Pinned Posts**
These are the first 3 things a new visitor reads. They need to be a mini-funnel:
- **Pin 1: Founder Story.** Who are you and why do you do this? Build trust.
- **Pin 2: Business Intro.** What do you do and who is it for? Compel action.
- **Pin 3: Top-Performing Post.** What’s your best content? Reinforce credibility.

Crucially, the CTA in Pin 2 must match the CTA in your link in bio. They have to work together.

**4. Your Highlights**
Are they clean, branded, and current? Or a random collection of old stories? Keep it to 5–7 max. Recommended for service businesses: About, Results, Services, Reviews, Contact.

**5. Your Grid**
Zoom out and look at your last 9 posts. Does it look like a premium, trustworthy brand? Or is it a random mix of colors, fonts, and low-quality images? A polished grid communicates a polished brand.

Fix those five things, and you’ll have a profile that’s built to convert, not just to exist.
